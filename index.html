Frequently Asked Questions [FAQ]

Please refer to this before asking for help on our discord server.

As of 19/2/2024, Aimmy is not, and is not designed to be suitable for these games:

- Valorant
- Fortnite (HWID Ban)
- Apex Legends
- Warzone

Same goes for the following Anti-Cheats:

- EAC (Easy Anti-Cheat)
- BE (Battle-Eye)

Aimmy is not, and will never be designed to run on any particular games, and despite any updates we make to Aimmy, we cannot, and will not ever make any guarantees that Aimmy will be undetected in any particular game. We are not responsible for any bans that occur due to Aimmy, that is your responsibility for using our product.

Aimmy is a passion project founded by a developer and a designer loved in their respective communities willing to venture into AI. As the project is a passion of ours and not a business, it is not in our best interests to make as much money as possible from this project. 

Instead, what we serve to gain by developing Aimmy is lots of kind words <3 and a chance to help people aim better.

Aimmy is not a free project to run, any of our expenses come right out of our own pockets. However, since Aimmy utilizes free services like GitHub for hosting, and is developed / run by, our operating costs are very low, meaning we currently do not have to resort to an ad-supported model to stay afloat.

While donations are not required for us to survive, we do accept donations, and it does mean a lot to us if you do! If you would like to donate to Aimmy, please do so below.

Aimmy is a product that utilizes AI to detect enemies. As such, it will use a significant portion of your computer's gaming power in order to perform this task. AI is not an easy task for a computer to perform after all.

Aimmy works best on Desktop PCs with a dedicated Mid-Range Graphics Card from 2016 or later. Such PCs can currently be found between $500-$1000 usd, if you can save up for one, we highly recommend it.

To give you a baseline for a Budget PC that works on Aimmy, we've devised a PC Part Picker list that you can take some inspiration from. Please note that we cannot confirm the safety of the AliExpress sellers where some of these items come from.
